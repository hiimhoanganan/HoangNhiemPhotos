ABOUT

THE PHOTOGRAPHER

Hoang The Nhiem

Born in 1960 in Ho Chi Minh City - Vietnam.
Lives and works in Ho Chi Minh City
Member of HOPA (HochiMinh Photography Association.)
VAPA (Vietnam Association of Photographic Artists.)
A.FIAP (Artist of the International Association of Photographic Arts)

Hoang The Nhiem is an excellent photographer in the landscape photography category of Vietnam. He is a beautiful, unique photographer of Sapa after the generation of An Ninh martial arts. He has had many photo exhibitions both domestically and internationally such as USA, France, Belgium, England ...
