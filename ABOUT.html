LIFE’S STORY

Borned in 1960 in HochiMinh city-VietNam.
Lives and works in HochiMinh city.
Hoang The Nhiem (Nhiem Hoang) is famous for his beautiful landscape photos. In 1994, he quit his job in the maritime telecommunications industry to pursue a career in photography. For him, landscape photography is the job that describes the beauty of nature. To capture moments that people have never seen before, he has really adventure to explore on his photography trips. Photographer Hoang The Nhiem is also one of the first to use Sony Alpha. After 25 years of shooting and exploring all regions of the country, his works show that an advanced camera with a high-quality image sensor in a compact body is a powerful companion for photographer. Good equipment will allow photographers to broaden their creative minds and unleash their full potential.
